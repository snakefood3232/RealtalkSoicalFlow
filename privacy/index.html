Privacy Policy

Effective Date: September 18, 2025

Who we are

RealTalk Social Flow (“we,” “our,” “us”) provides tools to connect social accounts, schedule posts, and view engagement. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect data.

Information we collect

  • Account info: name, email, and basic profile data from connected providers (e.g., Pinterest, Facebook, X, LinkedIn, Google).
  • Content data: posts you create, schedule, or manage.
  • Usage data: logs, device/browser info, and basic analytics.

How we use information

  • Provide core features (scheduling, publishing, analytics).
  • Improve the service and personalize your experience.
  • Send transactional messages (security notices, service updates).

Sharing

We do not sell data. We share data only with connected social networks to perform requested actions, and with service providers (hosting, email, payments) acting on our behalf.

Security

Access tokens and sensitive data are stored securely and encrypted where possible. You can disconnect an account at any time to revoke our access.

Retention

We keep data only as long as necessary to provide the service or comply with law. You can request deletion at any time.

Data deletion

To delete your data, visit https://realtalksocialflow.com/delete-data or email support@realtalksocialflow.com.

Updates

We may update this policy. Material changes will be posted here.
